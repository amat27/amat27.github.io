Consistency Spectrum
Weak Consistency:

- Eventual Consistency (Cassandra, Dynamo, Voldemort)
- Per-key Sequential
- CRDTs (Commutative Replicated Data Types)
- Red-blue Consistency
- Causal Consistency

Strong Consistency:

- Sequential
- Linearizability
- Transaction ACID (Hyperdex, Spanner, Transaction Chains)
